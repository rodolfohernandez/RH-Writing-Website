About

I’m a writer and entrepreneur currently working on building a legacy. Writing and hustling are two things that I’ve been doing my whole life, so one day I decided to leave the comfortable corporate world and pursue these passions. Making money is cool and something I definitely want to do, but not at the cost of my happiness or my creative energy. I would rather use these as fuel for my own dream machine so that I can then help the community power their own.

As a kid (a nerdy one too), I enjoyed playing with Lego products. I loved to build things, and as I got older, I’ve learned to express that interest through more abstract means. Now, I’m into building websites, brands, and companies from the ground up. Just like I did with those Lego bricks as a kid. Outside of these projects, I take trips back to Plato’s cave in order to unchain anyone who decides that the illusions in the shadows aren’t for them.
