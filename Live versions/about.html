About

I’m an analyst by trade, entrepreneur by spirit, and writer by birth that's currently working on building a legacy. I’ve been hustling my whole life, but I always felt that I was limiting my potential. So one day in 2015, I decided that I needed a break from the corporate world in order to reevaluate my goals and refocus my creative energy. Making money is cool and something I definitely want to do, I just feel like I should do so with purpose.

As a kid, I loved playing with Legos. I enjoyed building things, and as I got older, I learned how to express that interest through more abstract means. Now I’m into building websites, brands, and other projects from the ground up. However, I also leave room in my life to contribute to different organizations that could use a boost. Outside of these endeavors, I find time to visit Plato’s cave in order to unchain anyone who decides that the illusions in the shadows aren’t for them anymore. The world still needs a lot of work, but I'm optimistic that I can create a positive change.
